    About

    I am a landscape and wildlife photographer, located in The Wirral & Birmingham (previously Yorkshire), but also photographs The Lake District as well. I enjoy getting out and about taking photos of a wide range of scenes, and always love to challenge myself to come up with new creative ideas! Not only do I enjoy landscape and wildlife photography, but I also test myself to be creative and dip in and out of many other photography styles including flower photography, portrait and candid photography, as well as occasionally astrophotography.

    When not behind the camera, I can often be found exploring remote locations, being both unique and having a natural splendor! Accompanying this desire to explore is the drive and creativity involved in my Electronic Engineering degree, which I am currently completing at the University of York! I like to think that my approach to photography is to give a faithful representation of what is witnessed at each location, producing photographs which capture a natural essence and accurately portray the atmosphere & character of the scene.
